# Vision statement
## Vision statement
> Focus: next 20-40 years

Diffuse the merits of frontier technology to all of India, closing the knowledge divide between the haves and have-nots in India. Make frontier tech accessible to all.

## Mission statement
> Focus: next 2-3 years

Close the arbitrage between people who wield powers to AI tools and give it in its easiest-to-consume productized version to people who deserve to be empowered by this technology.

We don't work at the frontier - we work against it. We productize and apply what the top labs in the world are learning about tech. Our job is deployment, adoption and benefitting real people doing real things.

# About Us
HQ'd in Uttar Pradesh, India, which happens to be the most populous state in the most populous country of the world, Karwaan Labs is on the mission of democratizing access to frontier technology and building things that enable agency across 1.5 Billion Indians of the country.

There are people who choose to go to SF and work on the frontier. There are people who gel in with the builders of Bangalore and work from there. We share a different perspective. I'll beat an obvious one: real problems are solved on the ground, talking to real users. The culture in India does not have to be toxic to be productive. Getting the merits of frontier tech to the average consumer is not simply an engineering problem or a marketing problem. It's a design problem which needs to account for not only the technology being used but also the people using it. We operate in the following three modes:

1. We write posts describing a state of technology and how it shapes the cultural narrative of the country through masaladew.com.
2. We like software along with our friendly agents that gives superpowers to people so that they could build game-changing businesses out of India, taking leverage of frontier tech like AI agents and robotics. Read more about our software here.
3. We like checks and make accusations to accelerate tech transformation of vertical legacy software businesses and grow them beyond imagination, helping them be better and scaling them indefinitely. Learn more about our text vertical here.

We work in a remote-first setting with frequent retreats with our friendly agents, who are in some ways part-time employees to us. We observe the following fundamental truths. These are axioms that we stand by, even though we may share differing opinions on the paths to get there. The destination for us is the same:

1. Wealth can be generated besides being transferred and tech allows us to do that quickly and efficiently.
2. Profit is oxygen for our business. We don't take leverage for vanity and for publicity.
3. Honesty and transparency are paramount in building software.
4. Everything is infinitely abundant or can be made that way through technology, except for time and attention.
5. Progress is both growth and active maintenance of what already exists.
